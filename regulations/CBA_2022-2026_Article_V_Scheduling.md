# CBA 2022–2026 — Article V (Scheduling), Verbatim-Auszug

**Quelle:** MLB-MLBPA Basic Agreement 2022–2026 (offiziell, MLBPA),
https://registrationz.mlbpa.org/pdf/MLB%20Basic%20Agreement%202022-26.pdf
**Extrahiert:** Sprint 5 (Datengrundlage 5.2). Nur die scheduling-relevanten
Klauseln aus Article V(C). Zitate wortwörtlich; Auslassungen mit […] markiert.

> Verbindlichkeit: Dies ist der maßgebliche, aktuell gültige Wortlaut. Jede harte
> Scheduling-Regel im Optimierer sollte hierauf zeigen.

## V(C)(4) — Pre-Season Off-Day
> „There shall be one off-day with no workouts scheduled either after a Club breaks
> Spring Training camp and before the final pre-season exhibition game, or between
> the last Spring Training or pre-season exhibition game and the first championship
> season game. […]"

## V(C)(5)–(7) — Startzeiten Day Games / Doubleheaders
> (5) „a game will not be scheduled to start after 5 P.M. if either Club is scheduled
> to play a day doubleheader the next day […]"
> (6) „Day games shall not be scheduled or […] rescheduled to start before 1 P.M.,
> except […] between Noon and 1 P.M., if each Club meets one of the following […]:
> (a) if an off-day occurred the previous day; or (b) if a game were played in the
> same city within the previous 24 hours."

## V(C)(8) — Getaway-Game Startzeit (koppelt an TV!)
> „The latest possible start time for getaway games on days when the visiting Club
> travels to a home off-day or either Club travels to another game the following day
> shall be determined by taking the portion of the in-flight time that exceeds
> 2 1/2 hours, and subtracting that amount of time from 7 P.M. However, the foregoing
> sentence **shall not apply to getaway games that are broadcast on ESPN's 'Sunday
> Night Baseball'** or rescheduled games. […] All references to 'in-flight time' in
> this Article V shall be as set forth in Appendix C."

## V(C)(9) — Tag-nach-Nacht in anderer Stadt
> „No Club shall be scheduled or rescheduled to start a game prior to 5 P.M. when one
> of the Clubs played a game the prior evening in a different city with a start time
> of 7 P.M. or later, except as follows: (a) the in-flight time […] is 1 1/2 hours or
> less and the day game is on a holiday or is a home opener; (b) up to six instances
> per season where the traveling Club is traveling to Chicago to play the Cubs […];
> or (c) the day game is a rescheduled game with an in-flight time of 1 1/2 hours or
> less […]."

## V(C)(11) — Pacific → Eastern Time Zone (harter Off-Day)
> „An open day shall be scheduled where travel from cities in the Pacific Time Zone to
> cities in the Eastern Time Zone is required except that the Office of the Commissioner
> may schedule up to seven games per championship season in each League with a starting
> time after 7 P.M. in the Eastern Time Zone which include a Club that the day before
> played a game scheduled to start prior to 5 P.M. in the Pacific Time Zone. In any
> championship season, however, no Club may be scheduled to play more than one game in
> the Eastern Time Zone the day after it has played a game in the Pacific Time Zone."

## V(C)(12) — Konsekutive Spieltage (DIE zentrale Workload-Regel)
> „No Club shall be scheduled, or rescheduled if practicable, to play **more than
> twenty consecutive dates without an open day**. A rained-out game may be rescheduled
> to an open date in the same series, or to an open date at the end of the same series,
> if: (a) the open date is a road off-day for the visiting Club, and (b) the
> rescheduling does not result in the home team playing **more than twenty-four
> consecutive dates** without an open day."

## V(C)(13) — Off-Day-Verteilung
> „[…] a Club shall not be scheduled for **more than two open days in any seven-day
> period**. All Clubs will be scheduled for at least **seven open days over the final
> 67 days** of the championship season, at least **three** of which will be scheduled
> over the final 32 days […]."

## V(C)(14)/(15) — Doubleheader
> (14) „Doubleheaders shall not be scheduled on consecutive dates in the original
> schedule. The original schedule may contain one home split doubleheader for each Club."
> (15) „Twi-night doubleheaders will be limited in the original schedule to three per
> home Club per season. A twi-night doubleheader will not be scheduled on a getaway day."

## V(C)(17) — All-Star-Break
> „The All-Star break will contain **four days**, during which time Club championship
> season games shall not be played. […] No Club shall be scheduled to play the Sunday
> night prior to the All-Star Game more than once during the term of the Basic
> Agreement […]."

## V(C)(18) — Waiver
> „[…] any scheduling or rescheduling rule set forth in this Article V may be waived by
> the **secret ballot vote of a majority of the Players** on the Club(s) that would be
> in violation of the rule. […]"
